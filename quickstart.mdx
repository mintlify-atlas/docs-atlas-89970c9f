## User Guide

This guide will walk you through the main features of Por el Barrio as a user.

## First Launch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the App">
    Launch Por el Barrio on your device. You'll land on the **Home** screen showing local places.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Your Location (Optional)">
    Tap the **Profile** tab to configure your preferences:
    
    - Enter your name
    - Select your province of interest
    - Select your municipality
    - Save changes
    
    This filters the home screen to show only places in your area.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Discovering Places

### Browse by Category

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/categories.png" alt="Category chips" />
</Frame>

The home screen displays a horizontal carousel of category chips:

- **Todos** - View all places
- **Restaurant** - Food and dining
- **Shop** - Retail stores  
- **Service** - Services and professionals

Tap any category to filter the list. The badge shows the count of places in that category.

### Scroll to Hide Categories

<Info>
  As you scroll down, the category bar automatically hides to give you more screen space. A chip appears in the header showing the active filter. Scroll up to reveal categories again.
</Info>

### View Place Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap a Card">
    Tap any place card on the home screen to see full details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Information">
    - View photos
    - Read description
    - See address and phone number
    - Check current offers
    - View location on map
    - Read customer reviews
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Rating & Reviewing

### Write a Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Place Details">
    Navigate to any place's detail page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Expand Review Form">
    Scroll down and tap **"Escribe tu opinión"** (Write your review).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Rate with Stars">
    Tap stars to select your rating (1-5).
    
    ```tsx
    1 ⭐ = Poor
    2 ⭐⭐ = Fair  
    3 ⭐⭐⭐ = Good
    4 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ = Very Good
    5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ = Excellent
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Comment (Optional)">
    Write a comment describing your experience (max 500 characters).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **"Publicar opinión"** to publish. Your review appears immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must have a name configured in your profile to submit reviews.
</Warning>

### View Reviews

On each place's detail page, scroll to see all customer reviews:

- Star rating
- Author name  
- Comment text
- Date posted

## Business Owners

### Add Your Business

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Colaboración Tab">
    Tap the **Colaboración** tab in the bottom navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Request Form">
    Tap **"Descargar Solicitud.xlsx"** to download the Excel form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Out Form">
    Complete all fields:
    - Business name
    - Category
    - Description  
    - Address
    - Phone number
    - Province & Municipality
    - Location coordinates
    - Photos (optional)
    - Offers (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send via WhatsApp">
    Tap **"Abrir WhatsApp"** to send the completed form to:
    
    **+53 527 086 02**
    
    The developer will review and add your business to the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Management

### Update Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profile Tab">
    Tap the **Profile** tab in the bottom navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Fields">
    - **Name**: Your display name for reviews
    - **Email**: Contact email (optional)
    - **Province**: Your province of interest  
    - **Municipality**: Your municipality (filtered by province)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Tap **"Guardar cambios"** to save. The home screen will update to show places in your selected area.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Guest Mode

<Info>
  You can use the app without authentication. Your profile data is stored locally on your device with a unique guest ID.
</Info>

## Navigation

The app uses a drawer + tabs layout:

### Bottom Tabs

- **Inicio** (Home) - Browse places
- **Detalles** (Details) - View place details (context-based)
- **Colaboración** (Collaboration) - Add your business  
- **Profile** - Manage your profile

### Drawer Menu (Swipe from left)

- Filter by category
- Access settings
- View about information

## Tips & Tricks

<Tip>
  **Quick Category Switch**: Use the drawer menu to quickly jump to a specific category from anywhere in the app.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Refresh Data**: Pull down on the home screen to refresh the list of places.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Map View**: Tap the map on a place's detail page to open it in your device's maps app.
</Tip>
